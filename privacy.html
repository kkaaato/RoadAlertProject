Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

RoadAlert ("we", "our", or "us") is a community-powered road monitoring platform serving the Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using RoadAlert, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information — your Gmail address and chosen username when you register.
  • Location data — your approximate GPS coordinates, used only when you choose to place a road report or find your location on the map. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Road reports — the type, description, coordinates, and timestamp of any report you submit.
  • Votes — records of which reports you have upvoted or downvoted, linked to your account ID.
  • Device information — basic browser/device type collected automatically for error logging purposes only.
We do not collect your full name, phone number, payment details, or any sensitive personal information.

How Data Is Stored

All user data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform that is SOC 2 compliant and uses industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest).

Your data is stored on servers that may be located outside the Philippines. By using RoadAlert, you consent to this transfer.

Road reports are automatically deleted when they expire based on the duration you selected when submitting them.

Third-Party Services

RoadAlert uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Supabase — database and authentication. Privacy Policy
  • Leaflet.js — open-source map rendering library. No personal data is sent to Leaflet.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tile provider. Tile requests may include your IP address. Privacy Policy
  • Esri / ArcGIS — satellite map tiles (optional view). Privacy Policy
  • CARTO — dark mode map tiles (optional view). Privacy Policy
  • Font Awesome & cdnjs — icon fonts loaded from CDN. No personal data collected.

We do not sell your data to any third party, and none of these services receive your email address.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — you can view all reports you have submitted in the "My Reports" tab.
  • Delete reports — you can delete any of your own road reports at any time directly from the map.
  • Delete your account — to permanently delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. We will process your request within 14 days.
  • Data portability — you may request a copy of your stored data by contacting us.
To exercise any of these rights, email us at: support@roadalert.app

Cookies & Local Storage

RoadAlert does not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's local storage solely to keep you logged in between sessions. No advertising or analytics cookies are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of RoadAlert after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
